About Me

Profile Summary

Hi, I’m Marwan Putra Haji Husin. I’m an Electrical and Instrumentation Engineer with hands-on experience in smelter processing and industrial automation. I graduated from the Metal Industry Polytechnic of Morowali, majoring in Electric and Installation Engineering, and have been working in the field ever since.

For almost 3 years, I’ve been part of PT Dexin Steel Indonesia at the Indonesia Morowali Industrial Park (IMIP). My work mostly involves maintaining and repairing electrical systems and instrumentation —especially during smelting, ladle handling, continuous casting, and slag recycling. I’ve also handled crane troubleshooting and supported various field operations in the steelmaking process.

Along the way, I’ve earned certifications including Class 2 Prime Mover Operator, Industrial Automation Technician, and Occupational Safety and Health Expert.

Right now, I’m also studying Computer Science online at BINUS University to expand my tech and automation skills further. I’m passionate about engineering, technology, and safety, always eager to learn and collaborate.
